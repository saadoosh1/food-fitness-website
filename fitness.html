Fitness Tips

Staying active is an essential part of a balanced lifestyle. Whether you’re just beginning your fitness journey or looking to maintain momentum, the following tips can help you build strength, improve endurance, and support overall wellbeing.

  • Warm up and cool down: Always start your workouts with light cardio and stretching to prepare your muscles, and finish with a cool down to aid recovery.
  • Mix cardio and strength: Combine aerobic exercises (like walking, running, or cycling) with strength training to build endurance and muscle tone.
  • Listen to your body: Rest is just as important as activity. Incorporate rest days and pay attention to signs of overtraining.
  • Stay consistent: Short, regular sessions are often more effective than sporadic intense workouts. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise per week.
